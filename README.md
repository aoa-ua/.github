## What is AOA

AOA (an acronym for *Act of Attraction*) is a web-based social platform that brings social back offline. Instead of swipes and endless chats, AOA shows you who is **actually around** — at a café, in a coworking space, or at an event you just checked into. One tap, one signal, and the rest happens face-to-face.

> We are constantly online, but rarely together. AOA brings back the moment when you raise your eyes and meet someone's gaze.

## How it works

Three actions. One moment.

1. **Open the map** — venues, events, and places nearby in one place.
2. **Walk in** — at a venue, tap your phone to the NFC sticker on the table; at an event, check-in on arrival. You're now in the room.
3. **Send a honk** — a one-tap signal of interest. If they honk back, walk over.

No public profiles. No in-app chat. Visibility is bounded by your physical presence at a specific venue or event.

## AOA Plus & AOA Match

A subscription tier that adds:

- **Venue room visibility before arrival** — see how many guests are at a place and what they're looking for (open to meet, looking for company, networking).
- **AOA Match** — one daily curated meeting suggestion: **1-on-1** or a **table of four**, in a partner venue nearby.
- **Quiet-hour bonuses** at partner venues — discount on a second drink, a dessert with the main, a drink upgrade, or a fixed-price set for two.

| 🔁 **Club** | Recurring meetings (run clubs, book clubs) — 400+ repeat visits per year |

## Privacy & security

Privacy by default. No public profiles, no search-engine-indexed user pages. Authentication via Google, Apple, or Passkeys (WebAuthn). NFC NTAG424 DNA tags with cryptographic CMAC signatures prevent remote spoofing — only physically present guests can join a venue's room.
